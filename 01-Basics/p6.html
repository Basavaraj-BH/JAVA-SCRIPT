OPERATORS

Javascript Operators are symboles that are used to preform operations on operands.

        1. Arthematic operators => Addition +, Subtraction -, Mutiplication *, Division /, Modulus %, Increment ++, Decrement --.

        2. Comprison (Relational) Operators => Is equal to ==, Identical (equal and of same data type) ===, not equal to !=, Not Identical !==, Greator than >, Greator equal to >=, less than <, Less than or equal to <=

        3. Bitwise Operator => Bitwise and &, Bitwise OR|, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise Left Shift <<, Bitwise right Shift >>, Bitwise right Shift with zero >>>

        4. Logical Operators => Logical AND &&, Logical OR ||, Logical Not!

        5.Assignment Operators =. Assign =, Add and assign +=, subtract and assign -=, multiply and assign *=, Divide and ssign /=, Modulus and assign %=

        6. special or ternary Operators => condition ? "true":"false";
